Interactive Features

  • Feature 1: Allow students to list items for sale with details like price, category, and condition.
  • Feature 2: Enable buyers to search, filter, and sort items by category, price, or proximity.
  • Feature 3 A feedback system where users can rate their transaction experience.

Data and Impact

The app will store user-generated listings and feedback using a database, ensuring data persistence. It will address the problem of affordability and accessibility for UW students, offering a hyper-localized marketplace.
